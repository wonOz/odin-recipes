The Chickenbake Sandwich

back home

The Chickenbake Sandwich takes the classic Costco entre between bread, in stead of its normal doughy encasing.

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Chicken Breast
  • Bacon
  • Gouda Cheese
  • Parm Cheese

Steps

  1. Cut bread in half
  2. Toss ingredients in
  3. Toast it, highly recommend
  4. Smash it!
