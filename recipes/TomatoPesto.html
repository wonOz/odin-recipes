The Tomato Pesto Sandwich

back home

One of the most refreshing sandwiches on the list. The Tomato Pesto is a classic and a great place to start modifying. I suggest adding any meat of your choice and doubling the cheese.

Ingredients

  • Chiabatta Bread
  • Sun Dried Tomatos
  • Sprouts
  • Meat of choice: Chicken Breast, Bacon, Peppered Turkey
  • Swiss Cheese
  • Pesto Spread

Steps

  1. Spread generous amount of Pesto spread on both sides of bread.
  2. Place Sun Dried Tomatos over the "top" slice of bread.
  3. Place meat on "bottom" bread
  4. Place Swiss Cheese slize over meat on "bottom" slice.
  5. Place two sides in toaster over and heat all that up.
  6. Place Sprouts over Tomatos on "bottom" slice.
  7. Smash those two sides together and enjoy!
